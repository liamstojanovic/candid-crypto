### Summary

Crypto has always been risky, but recent failures of the most prominent crypto exchanges (FTX) and projects have made everyone aware that it is easy to lose your digital assets. Scams have never been more prevalent in the crypto space, which is why it is necessary to know the playbook of these fraudulent actors. In this episodes, Michael and Liam review the most common crypto scams and how to avoid them.
