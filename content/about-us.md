## What is Candid Crypto?

Candid Crypto’s mission is to empower beginners to learn about cryptocurrency and blockchain technology. Our core belief is that Bitcoin and programmable blockchains spawned a new type of digital literacy, one that intersects financial know-how with software. “Digital cash” has proven to be far more versatile beyond acting as a store of value – [governance](https://www.investopedia.com/terms/o/onchain-governance.asp), [proof of ownership](https://ethereum.org/en/nft/), [AMM](https://www.gemini.com/cryptopedia/amm-what-are-automated-market-makers)s, and [more](https://decrypt.co/resources/blockchain-basics-what-is-blockchain) – have emerged from the [whitepaper](https://bitcoin.org/bitcoin.pdf) Satoshi Nakamoto published in 2008.

Our goal is to enable anyone to learn about, and benefit from, blockchain tech.
